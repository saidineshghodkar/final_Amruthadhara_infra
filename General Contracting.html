End-to-End Construction Management with Precision

At Amruthadhara Infra, our General Contracting services provide complete oversight of your construction project—from initial planning to final handover. We manage every aspect of site execution, ensuring quality, safety, timelines, and budgets are strictly adhered to.

With our disciplined, hands-on approach, we bring technical expertise and practical site knowledge to deliver projects that meet the highest standards of construction excellence.

Our Key Services

Project Planning

Detailed scheduling, resource allocation, and timeline development for efficient project execution.

Site Management

Skilled labor coordination, subcontractor management, and daily progress monitoring.

Quality Control

Rigorous material testing, workmanship checks, and compliance verification at every stage.

Cost Management

Budget tracking, cost optimization, and transparent financial reporting throughout the project.

Our 5-Step Process

1

Site Assessment & Planning

We begin with a thorough site visit to understand terrain, accessibility, local conditions, and constraints. This ground-truthing forms the basis of our realistic planning.

  • Topographic and soil analysis
  • Accessibility and logistics planning
  • Regulatory and compliance review
2

Detailed Planning & Scheduling

We create comprehensive project plans with realistic timelines, resource allocation, and milestone tracking. Our planning includes risk assessment and mitigation strategies.

  • Work breakdown structure development
  • Resource and manpower planning
  • Critical path method scheduling
3

Execution & Monitoring

With teams mobilized and resources in place, we execute the plan with daily supervision. We maintain strict quality controls and regular progress reporting.

  • Daily site supervision and coordination
  • Progress tracking against milestones
  • Real-time problem solving and adjustments
4

Quality Assurance

Multiple checkpoints ensure material quality and workmanship meet our standards. We conduct regular inspections and testing throughout construction.

  • Material testing and certification
  • Workmanship quality audits
  • Safety compliance verification
5

Timely Handover

We ensure complete documentation and operational readiness before project handover. Our process includes final inspections, snag lists, and client orientation.

  • Final inspections and snag resolution
  • Complete documentation delivery
  • Client orientation and training
