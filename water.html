Securing Water Resources for Climate Resilience

Our Water & Climate Solutions division specializes in engineering-led approaches to water security, flood management, and climate adaptation. We combine hydrological science with practical site knowledge to create sustainable water infrastructure.

From rainwater harvesting to flood mitigation, our solutions are designed to address India's water challenges while building climate resilience for communities, agriculture, and industry.

Our Water & Climate Solutions

Rainwater Harvesting

Complete rainwater collection, storage, and utilization systems for residential, commercial, and industrial applications.

  • Rooftop rainwater harvesting
  • Surface runoff capture systems
  • Custom storage and filtration

Groundwater Recharge

Artificial recharge techniques to replenish aquifers through recharge wells, percolation tanks, and infiltration basins.

  • Recharge well construction
  • Percolation tank design
  • Aquifer mapping & assessment

Flood Management

  • Urban drainage systems

  • Watershed management

  • Decentralized treatment plants

  • Resilience master planning

Our Water Solution Process

1

Water Audit & Site Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation of water sources, usage patterns, site topography, and climate data to understand water challenges.

  • Water source identification and testing
  • Rainfall pattern and runoff analysis
  • Soil infiltration and water table studies
2

Solution Design & Engineering

Hydrological modeling and engineering design of integrated water systems tailored to site-specific conditions and client needs.

  • Hydraulic calculations and system sizing
  • Material selection and specification
  • Climate resilience integration
3

Implementation & Construction

Precise execution of water infrastructure with quality control, safety protocols, and minimal environmental disruption.

  • Earthwork and excavation for storage structures
  • Pipeline and conveyance system installation
  • Treatment system construction and testing
4

System Integration & Testing

Commissioning of complete water systems with performance testing, quality checks, and operational verification.

  • Hydraulic pressure and flow testing
  • Water quality analysis and treatment validation
  • System integration and automation
5

Monitoring & Maintenance

Ongoing performance monitoring, maintenance protocols, and capacity building for sustainable water management.

  • Performance monitoring and data collection
  • Maintenance schedule and protocols
  • User training and capacity building
